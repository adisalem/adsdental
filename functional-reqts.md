## Office Manager
- Register new dentists
- Record dentist details (ID, name, phone, email, specialization)
- Enroll new patients
- Record patient details (name, phone, email, address, date of birth)
- Receive appointment requests (phone and online)
- Book appointments for patients
- Send appointment confirmation email to patients

## Patient
- Request appointment via online form
- Call to request appointment (handled by Office Manager)
- Sign in to the system
- View their appointments
- View assigned dentist details
- Request appointment cancellation
- Request appointment change

## Dentist
- Sign in to the system
- View all assigned appointments
- View patient details for scheduled appointments

## System
- Record and store appointments (date, time, patient, dentist, surgery)
- Maintain surgery information (name, address, phone)
- Send confirmation email upon booking
- Enforce maximum of 5 appointments per dentist per week
- Check for unpaid bills before allowing new appointment requests
- Prevent appointment requests if unpaid bills exist
